ANIMO models

This page contains the ANIMO models used in published papers. Most of the figures of those papers were produced directly in ANIMO. The linked directories contain .cys (Cytoscape session) files with the ANIMO models and, where available, the experimental data used as reference for the models is provided as .csv (comma separated values) files.
